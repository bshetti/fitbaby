Introducing the ultimate fitness app for babies

You want your baby to have the best chance at growing, developing and being happy.
Fitbaby gives you the information to know more about your babies well-being

Heartrate Monitor

Fitbaby monitors your babies heart rate. Continuously feeding information back to our data analytics engine for you to analyze on your phone.

Crawl Tracker

Where is your baby now? Fitbaby not ony tracks where your baby is, but we measure the calories burned on every crawl.

Calorie Counter

Calories are not only measured, but we also put together the estimated food intake and type of food your baby should be on to maintain their growth pattern..

Temperature Monitor

With continuous temperature monitoring, you'll know when your baby is potentially having a fever..
